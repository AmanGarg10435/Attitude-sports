About
Coach Gary has been teaching aspiring players, both casual and recreational, for 20 years and has never lost any of his passion. He has coached players from a wide variety of backgrounds and skill levels and will definitely be able to help you. He has an infectious passion for the sport that is evident in every lession he enthusiastically teaches. When Gary founded Attitude sports he was working alone, however now he has amassed a team of qualified instructors that each have their own unique skills and weaknesses and are able to help with many different aspects of the game.
Coaching
Our coaches utilize drills used by national level players to ensure our players progress at the fastest rate. We are able to tailer drills to players strengths and weakeness to ensure holistic development of our players. We offer both private and group training in order to allow our players to learn in a setting where they are most comfortable. to maximise improvement we reccomend a mix of group and private training to allow new techniques learned in private training to be implemented in group lesson sparring. Do not hesitate to contact us with your enquiries and attend a free trial group lesson.
